How long does it appear and/or action

Pending

Your application for financial assistance is currently being assessed.

 

Assessment can take up to 10 working days from submission of application.

 

You will be notified of the outcome by email.

 

Until their status changes to approved, rejected or more information required.

Approved

Your application for financial assistance has been approved.

 

Please refer to the confirmation email for more detail.

 

If you have any queries, please email membershipsupport@aat.org.uk

 

Until their next renewal is invoiced.

 

e.g. renewal date = 01/01/2014, invoice is raised in CRM/AX – 10/11/2013 – the message should appear from the 10/11/2013.

 

Rejected

No Message

Can reapply if they are within the 3 month window

 

If outside the 3 months then the “outside of the time period” applies.

 

Withdrawn

No Message

Can reapply if they are within the 3 month window

 

If outside the 3 months then the “outside of the time period” applies.

 

More information required

Thank you for application for financial assistance.

 

Further information has been requested from you to support your application.

 

Please refer to the confirmation email for more detail or contact us on 0845 863 0801

 

Until the status changes or the time period kicks in.

Outside of the time period to apply

You are no longer eligible to apply for financial assistance as applications must be received within 3 months of your membership renewal date.

 

If you have any further queries, please email memberhipsupport@aat.org.uk

 

Until their next renewal is invoiced.
