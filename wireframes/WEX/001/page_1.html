WORK EXPERIENCE  [WEX]

 

Submit your work experience

Work experience is a key requirement for AAT full membership. It demonstrates that you can apply your skills and knowledge in a practical work environment.

 

To meet this requirement you must be able to demonstrate competencies using examples from your work. These include:

 

• 7 personal effectiveness competencies

• 1 technical competency

 

You will need to submit your work experience using the links below.

Supporting information

Please read the following before you start:

 

•  Work experience introduction - what counts as work experience, how to select your verifier for the technical competency, and how work experience is assessed

 

•  Competency guide (PDF) -  outlines each work experience competency with examples of tasks you can carry out to achieve it 

Submitting your work experience

You must submit your work experience using the links below. You will need to complete all 7 personal effectiveness competencies, and choose 1 technical competency to complete.

 

With each example you will need the name of the organisation where you met each competence, your job title and the dates you worked there.

 

It should take approximately 20 minutes to describe what you have done to meet each competence. You can also save information and add more at a later time if you prefer. It may help to bookmark this page.
