Before you begin

 

 

You will need the following items to complete your registration

 

• Your email address

• The name of your AAT Approved training provider - Need to find a training provider?

• Your Unique Learner Number (ULN) or Scottish Candidate Number (SCN)

• A valid payment code (provided by your training provider) or credit/debit card details.

• We accept Mastercard, Visa, Visa Debit, and Maestro. We do not accept American Express or Diners cards

• If you have ever been an AAT student or member please contact our Membership Support team

 

The registration process will take approximately 15 minutes.
