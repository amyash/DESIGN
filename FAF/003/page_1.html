We acknowledge there are occasions where our members may need short term support to retain their membership with the AAT.

 

There is now one new financial assistance process for members that meet the eligibility criteria can apply for assistance through. This replaces the previous reduced subscription and Granting assistance applications.

 

Depending on your personal circumstances, you may be eligible to apply for financial assistance towards your membership fees and/or assessment fees. Please note this assistance is only available to you if you are responsible for paying your own fees.

Applicants will be asked to submit evidence to support their application. The category chosen will determine the information that will be requested and the amount of financial assistance which may be offered.

 

Please note – this is short term financial assistance for members that pay their own fees.  Applicants will need to re-apply each year if their personal circumstance, to which the application is made on, has not changed.

 

Applications must be received within three months of the membership renewal date to be considered.
